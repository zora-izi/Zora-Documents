# Delete your Zora account

**Blank Page Digital Ltd** operates **Zora** on iOS and Android.

You can permanently delete your Zora account and the personal data we hold on our servers. Deletion cannot be undone.

---

## Option 1 — In the App (recommended)

1. Open **Zora** and sign in.
2. Go to **Settings**.
3. Tap **Delete account**.
4. Confirm, then verify your identity:
   - **iOS:** Sign in with Apple
   - **Android:** Sign in with Google

This deletes your cloud account data and clears most local data on that device.

---

## Option 2 — Email request

If you cannot access the App (for example, you are on the wrong platform for your sign-in provider), email us:

**hello@blankpagedigital.app**

Send from the email address linked to your Apple or Google account, or include enough detail for us to verify your identity. We will process verified requests within a reasonable time.

---

## What deletion removes

From our production systems:

- Account profile and sign-in identity
- Synced nutrition, weight, water, fasting, and daily feel logs
- Related usage records tied to your account

On your device, most local app data is cleared. Basic display preferences and a non-personal installation identifier may remain.

---

## What deletion does **not** do

- **Cancel Zora Pro** — cancel your subscription in **Apple ID → Subscriptions** or **Google Play → Subscriptions**.
- **Remove data instantly from all backups** — encrypted backups may be retained for up to **30 days** as described in our [Privacy Policy](https://zora-izi.github.io/Zora-Documents/privacy-policy).

---

## Cross-platform note

- An **Apple** account generally cannot be deleted from the Android App.
- A **Google** account generally cannot be deleted from the iOS App.

Use the email option above if you are on the wrong platform.
